Introduction

For ENGL 1102's first major project, I presented a monologue as a challenge to myself and as a change of pace from the rest of the class. The story is a shortened retelling of the events of my grandmother's funeral, a critical time in my life and an example of my childhood experiences with my Vietnamese heritage.
