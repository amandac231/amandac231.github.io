Introduction

The purpose of the Common First Week Video was to introduce myself to the class. Given the class' theme of genre and media, I decided to do an homage to one of my favorite genres: science fiction. My video is meant to mimic the captain's log entries of Star Trek's Captain Kirk.

Common First Week Video

Script

Captain's Log: Stardate 99643.1

We have begun our course designated ENGL 1102 under Dr. S's supervision with a class specialization in genre. The class is being attended in the Spring semester of the participant's first year on the campus known as Georgia Tech. The participant is from a small area called Monroe, Georgia, and has chosen to major in physics. The anticipated challenges of the course are most likely going to be with the oral and electronic methods of communication. Despite the participant's involvement in high school theater, she has some levels of stage fright that result in stuttering. The participant also struggles with learning technology, despite her enrollment in a tech school. All future challenges are expected to be overcome by the end of the semester. In spite of the struggles of last semester, I do not believe in the no-win scenario, and I will persevere.

Amanda Cowan, out.
